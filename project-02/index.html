Grandma's Christmas Cookies

Indgredients

  • 1 cup room temp. butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar, sifted
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 vanilla bean* (or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract)
  • 2 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour, spooned and leveled
  • 1 cup almond flour, spooned and leveled
  • ½ cup jam of your choice


Directions

  1. In a large bowl mix butter for about 1-3 minutes until creamy.
  2. Add each ingreadient in order until combined. Start with sugar, egg yolks, salt, cinnamon, vanilla, and lemon juice.
  3. Stir in both types of flour.
  4. Divide the dough intwo two. Wap tightly in a plastic wrap, and chill it in the fridge for a couple of hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 350℉ Line 2 or 3 baking sheets with parchment paper. Set aside.
  6. Roll out dough into a 1/4 inch thickness on a lightly floured surface. Cut out cookies with a 2-inch cookie cutter of your choice and place on the prepared baking sheets with about 1-2 inch space in-between.
  7. Bake each sheet of cookes for about 9-10 minutes until the surface appears dry.
  8. Let them cool.
  9. Fill cookes with jam by spreading jam on one side of cookie and placing another one on top.
  10. Cookies taste best after being stored in a cool temperature for a few days.
