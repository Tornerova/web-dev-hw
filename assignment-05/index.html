Best Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 small avocado
  • 1/2 tsp. fresh lemon juice
  • 1/8 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 slice of whole grain bread, toast
  • 1/2 tsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • red pepper flakes for topping

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, mash avocado with the back of a fork.
  2. Combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Top toasted bread with avocado mixture.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil, and add desired topings/
