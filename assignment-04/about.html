About William Shakespeare

Born in mid 16th century,and living through the Elizabethan and Jacobean era, William Shakespeare is often regarded as the greatest english writer. Yet he was not just that, he was also playwringht, poet, and an actor. He wrote about 39 plays and 160 peomes. His plays have been translated to every major living language and to this day are more performed than any other playwrights. His work continues to be studied and interpreted. Sonnet 18 is one of his most famous poems with layers of meaning left for our interpretation.
